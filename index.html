Active Rules

  • No bench. Everyone is assigned a spot every inning.
  • Infield spots are Pitcher, 1st Base, 2nd Base, Shortstop, and 3rd Base.
  • All remaining players are spread across the outfield.
  • No one stays in the same field area two innings in a row unless the roster size forces it.
  • Pitcher, 1st, Shortstop, and 3rd are rotated fairly across games and the season.
  • Batting order rotates by saved game so the same player does not always lead off.

Fewer than 12 players: the outfield removes Right Deep first, then Left Deep. When deep spots are gone, the outside spots are labeled Left Field and Right Field.
