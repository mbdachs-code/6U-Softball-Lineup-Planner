League Share Guide

Coach Instructions

This planner builds inning-by-inning softball lineups, printable field cards, and season fairness tracking for each team.

Quick Start

  1. Enter your team name and choose your two team colors.
  2. Enter your full roster and click Save roster.
  3. Check only the players attending this game.
  4. Add opponent, game date, game time, and planned innings.
  5. Click Generate packet.
  6. After the game, change Innings actually played if needed, then click Save game to season.

Helpful Tips

  • Undo last save removes an accidental game save.
  • Export data saves your roster, branding, and season stats into one file.
  • Import data loads that file on another device or browser.
  • The batting-order page always shows 12 lines so you can hand-write a late arrival.

Sharing With Another Coach

  1. Click Export data.
  2. Send the exported `.json` file to the other coach.
  3. They open the app and click Import data.

iPad And iPhone

  1. Open the published site in Safari.
  2. Tap Share, then Add to Home Screen.
  3. Open the home-screen app once while online so it caches the files.
  4. After that, it can open without internet.
  5. Use Print / Save PDF to export the packet from the app.
